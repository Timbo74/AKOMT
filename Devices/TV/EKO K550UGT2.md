# EKO K550UGT2 

## Troubleshooting

### Factory Reset via Recovery Mode 
If the TV is locked up and you cannot access the on-screen settings, you can force a hard factory reset using the physical buttons and your remote. 

**Using the Remote:** 
- Unplug the TV from the wall socket.
- Press and hold the ```VOL+``` button on your remote.
- Continue holding the button and immediately plug the TV back into the power outlet.  Make sure the remote is pointed directly at the TV’s IR sensor.
- Wait for the recovery menu to appear on the screen (it will say ```"Entering recovery system! Erasing"```).
