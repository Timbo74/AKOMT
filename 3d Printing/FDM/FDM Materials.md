# FDM Materials

## PLA
**Polylactic Acid**  
The easiest to print, biodegradable, and rigid, but brittle with low heat resistance; ideal for visual models and beginners.

## ABS
**Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene**  
Tough, durable, and heat-resistant, but prone to warping and requires ventilation; suited for functional prototypes. 

## PETG
**Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol**  
Balances strength and flexibility with chemical resistance and ease of printing; excellent for containers and mechanical parts. 

## Nylon
Offers very high strength, impact resistance, and wear resistance, but is difficult to print due to moisture absorption; used for gears and hinges. 

## TPU
**Thermoplastic Polyurethane**  
A flexible, rubber-like material with high elasticity and abrasion resistance; perfect for phone cases and shock absorbers.
