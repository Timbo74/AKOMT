# Acronis

## EDR / XDR / MDR

**Reference:** 
https://www.acronis.com/en/blog/posts/edr-mdr-xdr-everything-you-need-to-know/

- EDR - Individual devices/aspect.
- XDR - Correlates many devices/aspects.
- MDR - Monitored by teams of people.

**EDR (Endpoint Detection and Response)** focuses solely on monitoring and securing individual devices like laptops and servers.

**XDR (Extended Detection and Response)** expands EDR by gathering and correlating threat data across your entire environment—including endpoints, network traffic, and cloud services.

**MDR (Managed Detection and Response)** is an outsourced service where human experts monitor your security tools 24/7.
